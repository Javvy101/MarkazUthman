What is Islam?

Islam calls to true monotheism founded on the teachings of the Prophet Muhammad صلى الله عليه وآله وسلم (peace & blessings be upon him). It is based on the Quran, The Divine Speech of our creator Allah, and the Sunnah (the teachings and practices) of the Prophet صلى الله عليه وآله وسلم .

Who is Allah?

Allah is the Arabic word for God, the one and only deity in Al-Islam. We as Muslims believe in the oneness of Allah. In his Lordship, In his Worship and in his Names & Attributes. Specific only to Allah alone.

What is the Sunnah?

The Sunnah refers to the practices, actions and sayings of Prophet Muhammad صلى الله عليه وآله وسلم (peace be upon him). In every aspect it provides guidance on how to live a correct, righteous, content and fulfilling life to attain the eternal abode of Paradise.
